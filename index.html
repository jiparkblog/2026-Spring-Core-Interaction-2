🪦 01.2026 — 05.2026

Core Interaction 2 Class ran January–May 2026. 14 students, 28 classes. We explored the web as a medium, a material with its own politics, aesthetics, and failure modes.

We prioritized process, iteration, and intentional experimentation over perfect outcomes. Breaking things was part of the work.

We read legends like Lialina, Steyerl, Haraway and worked through the history of the web, positive friction, AI Slops, AI ethics (yes, we talked about Palantir), and spent a lot of time asking what higher design education is actually for, who it serves, and what it leaves out. We didn't find an answer yet lol!
