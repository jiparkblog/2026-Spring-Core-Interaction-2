Core Interaction 2 is a studio course about building interactive systems that behave and misbehave.

We'll explore the web as a medium, not just a container for content. We prioritize process, iteration, and intentional experimentation.

We're here for breaking things on purpose, failed attempts, process over product, iteration as practice. Things will break and that's the work :)
